Features

  • Living Terrain

    Draw paths that reshape snow, melt ice, or grow forests depending on your choices and the ever-changing seasons.

  • Resource Wisdom

    You've got to manage your warmth, light, and energy - but if you're smart about your routes, you can make limited resources work for you.

  • Aurora Puzzles

    Align constellations, reflect light with ice, and harness the northern lights to unlock blocked routes.

  • Endless Discovery

    The world generates randomly but there are still these cool story spots. Each time I play feels different.
