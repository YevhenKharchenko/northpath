Features

  • Perspective Puzzles

    Shift your view to align disconnected paths and unlock impossible routes through optical illusions.

  • Tranquil Worlds

    Explore these gorgeous floating gardens and temple-like structures - the art style is super clean and minimalist, really easy on the eyes.

  • Gentle Mechanics

    Swipe or tap to glide your cube — no rushing around or stuff trying to kill you - just you, the puzzles, and that 'aha!' moment when things click.

  • Progressive Calm

    Simple starts evolve into layered challenges with switches, rotating platforms, and color shifts that keep you engaged without stress.
